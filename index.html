What is BlingChat

I know, a 'bot' might sound complicated, but it's not. BlingChat is a chat-based service that sits on top of Facebook Messenger and gives you engagement rings' inspiration and education. Click below for a "Bling" journey on Facebook Messenger and start chatting with me as if you are chatting with a friend (well, a bot-friend).

Show Your Rings

Designers? You can also submit your rings onto the bot and get discovered by the biggest jewelry market, Millennials. Millennials spend 30 minutes each day on Facebook Messenger, so it's the perfect platform for them and for you. All you need is to submit..well, your most beautiful, unique rings. Start by clicking below, no need to download an app, just start chatting.

Who Are We

Stav has been working in the diamond industry in NYC (but traveled all over) for the past 10 years and wanted to bring innovation into it. He proposed to his wife with a beautiful vintage ring, she loved it. Arik founded and own a leading crowdfunding platform, Mimoona (as seen on Bloomberg TV, Forbes and more), and created some fun side projects that got featured on the biggest tech blogs in the world. BlingChat is his second venture. Arik proposed to his wife with a ring he bought from her father (who works in diamond industry), of course it's beautiful, go get cheap buying a ring from her father ;). Yossi is our magician CTO, who started as a bio-chemistry engineer, but couldn't stop coding when he started 9 years ago. Yossi already developed bots on Facebook, Kik and Telegram. He was too busy coding, that he almost got scammed looking for engagement ring, but eventually a friend helped him find the one. She said "I do". We are here to take engagement ring discovery into a whole new level.
